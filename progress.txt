## Iteration 4 - 2026-05-14

### Completed Stories
- US-013: Click responses
  - Single click plays a `happy` petting reaction, then returns to the previous behavior.
  - Double click cancels pending petting and triggers `JumpBehavior`.
  - Right click opens the Electron pet context menu with Exit, Hide 30min, and Settings.
  - Drag starts only after pointer movement crosses a small threshold, so normal clicks do not trigger drag/fall first.
- US-014: Automatic behavior scheduler
  - Default weights are Idle 60 / Walk 30 / Jump 10.
  - Scheduler supports weighted distribution, jump cooldown, and pause after click/drag interaction.
  - Scheduler is integrated into `PetRuntime` and remains independent from concrete behavior classes.
- US-015: Config persistence
  - `ConfigStore` writes `config.json` under Electron `app.getPath('userData')`.
  - Config API supports `get`, `set`, and `onChange`.
  - Preload/config IPC exposes config to the renderer.
  - Idle, walk, jump, and fall behavior parameters read from runtime config.
